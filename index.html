New Zealand

The Māori are the indigenous Polynesian people of New Zealand. Although New Zealand is a civilized country, the Maori culture and language is well respected and celeberated by all the locals. As an honored guest or visitor to New Zealand, you can expect to be welcomed by a 'powhiri'(welcoming party) where you will witness a great 'haka'(traditional war dance/cry). A unique way that traditional Maori people greet is by 'hongi', which is where people press their noses together and breathe in or exchange breath.
